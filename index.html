Late night cakes

Baked in the dark, to brighten up your day

We bake custom decorated cupcakes in bulk for your special occasion. We also run a nocturnal cafe

Visit us! - We're located here...

If you're trucking around the area late at night or whatever you might be doing at that hour and you need a pick up - come by for a coffee and a cupcake. We're located just off the motorway near Arklow. For those who want to taste the produce before making a larger order for your function - drop by between 9 and 10am Monday to Saturday for cupcake tasters.
