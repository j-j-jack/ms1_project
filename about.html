Who are we?

In 2010 Richie Mulligan quit his job as a truck driver to open up a coffee and confectionary van called "Fast cakes". Along with his enthusiasm for good coffee, baking had been a long-time passion of his. So he sold his own cakes and creations on board the van. In 2014, encouraged by the popularity of his cupcakes in particular, he decided to open up Late night cakes - an all night cafe and bulk cupcake producer. Richie operated Speed cakes solo. Now, Late night cakes has 25 employees who work through the night to brighten up your day!

What do we do?

We produce custom cupcakes in bulk for whatever your function might be - from birthday parties to apocalypse parties! We bake through the night ready for pickup the following morning. This means that what you get is as fresh as it can possibly be. The cafe is opening from 9-10am daily for those who wish to taste some of the multitude of cupcakes we bake every night - before making an order for your special occasion. We also run an all-night cafe. As a former lorry driver, Richie still holds a place in his heart for those still on the road. The cafe runs from 9pm to 7am and is a great place to sit down and chat with other drivers while savouring a cup of fine coffee and some of the delicious cakes.

Why choose us?

We support local producers as much as we possibly can when buying our ingredients. 75% of our ingredients are sourced within 30km of our location. We have also won numerous awards for our cakes and Richie only hires those who have a true passion for baking. We supply for all types of occasions no matter how obscure or esoteric. You can order online or over the phone. If you want to make an order or query do not hesitate to fill out the form and somebody will reply to you via email asap.
